About Falasteen Trails

Falasteen Trails is dedicated to offering authentic and memorable travel experiences in Palestine. Our mission is to connect travelers with the rich history, culture, and natural beauty of the Holy Land while supporting local communities.

Company History

Falasteen Trails was founded in 2015 with a vision to showcase the rich cultural and historical heritage of Palestine. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted travel provider, serving thousands of travelers from around the world and building strong partnerships with local communities.

Our Mission

We aim to provide high-quality tours that combine cultural exploration, adventure, and comfort. Every tour is designed to highlight the unique heritage and landscapes of Palestine.
