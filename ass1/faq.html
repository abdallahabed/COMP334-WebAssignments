Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour by visiting the Booking page and filling out the form with your personal information, preferred tour, and dates.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Cancellations or rescheduling must be requested at least 7 days before the tour start date. Please contact us via email or phone for assistance.

Are group bookings available?

Yes, we offer group booking discounts. Contact us directly to organize tours for larger groups.

Tour Information

What should I bring on a tour?
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Water and snacks
  • Travel documents and identification
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
Are tours guided?

All tours are guided by professional local guides who provide historical, cultural, and practical insights.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, transportation within the tour itinerary is included unless stated otherwise in the package description.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and online payment platforms.

Are taxes and fees included in the tour price?

Yes, all listed prices include applicable taxes, accommodation, and guided services as described in each package.

Is a deposit required?

A 30% deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the remaining balance due 7 days before the tour starts.
